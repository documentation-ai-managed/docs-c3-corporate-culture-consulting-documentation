<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Tech Industry" icon="cpu">
    In the fast-paced tech sector, maintaining culture during rapid growth is critical for retention and innovation.
    
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Initial Assessment" icon="search">
        Conducted comprehensive culture surveys across all departments, revealing communication gaps as employees scaled.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Leadership Alignment" icon="users">
        Worked with executive team to define core values and leadership behaviors that would scale with growth.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Implementation" icon="settings">
        Launched company-wide initiatives including peer recognition programs and cross-functional collaboration tools.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Measurement" icon="bar-chart">
        Tracked engagement scores quarterly, achieving a 30% improvement while growing from 50 to 500 employees.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
    
    <Callout kind="success">The company reported 95% employee retention rate during the growth period and won "Best Place to Work" awards two years in a row.</Callout>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Manufacturing" icon="cog">
    Traditional industries often face unique cultural challenges related to change management and employee empowerment.
    
    <Expandable title="Challenge Identified" default-open="true">
      The organization experienced high turnover rates, particularly among younger workers who felt disconnected from company decision-making processes.
    </Expandable>
    
    <Expandable title="Solution Implemented" default-open="false">
      Introduced participatory management practices, employee suggestion programs, and transparent communication channels that gave workers a voice in operational improvements.
    </Expandable>
    
    <Expandable title="Results Achieved" default-open="false">
      Turnover decreased by 40%, productivity increased by 15%, and employee satisfaction scores rose significantly across all measured dimensions.
    </Expandable>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
