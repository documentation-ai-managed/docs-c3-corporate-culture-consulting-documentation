## Our Proven Methodology

C3's methodology combines research-backed frameworks with practical implementation strategies to ensure sustainable culture change. Our systematic approach addresses both the human and organizational elements of successful transformation, delivering measurable improvements in engagement, productivity, and retention.

<Tabs>
  <Tab title="Assessment Phase" icon="clipboard">
    Our assessment methodology provides a complete picture of organizational culture through multiple data sources and perspectives.
    
    <Steps>
      <Step title="Stakeholder Mapping" icon="users">
        Identify key groups and individuals who influence or are affected by organizational culture.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Data Collection" icon="database">
        Administer validated surveys, conduct focus groups, and review existing performance data.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Analysis" icon="bar-chart">
        Use statistical analysis and thematic coding to identify patterns and priorities.
      </Step>
      <Step title="Report Development" icon="file-text">
        Create comprehensive reports with actionable insights and prioritized recommendations.
      </Step>
    </Steps>
    
    <Callout kind="tip">We use a combination of quantitative metrics and qualitative insights to provide a complete cultural assessment.</Callout>
  </Tab>
  <Tab title="Design Phase" icon="lightbulb">
    During design, we translate assessment findings into practical, implementable strategies.
    
    <Expandable title="Value Alignment" default-open="true">
      Ensure all initiatives reinforce stated organizational values and support business objectives.
    </Expandable>
    
    <Expandable title="Change Sequencing" default-open="false">
      Prioritize initiatives based on impact potential and implementation feasibility, creating logical sequences of change.
    </Expandable>
    
    <Expandable title="Resource Planning" default-open="false">
      Identify required resources, timelines, and success metrics for each proposed initiative.
    </Expandable>
  </Tab>
</Tabs>
